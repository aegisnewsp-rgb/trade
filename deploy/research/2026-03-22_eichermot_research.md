## Business Description

Eicher Motors Limited manufactures motorcycles (Royal Enfield) and commercial vehicles through its Volvo Eicher commercial vehicle joint venture. Royal Enfield is the dominant mid-size motorcycle brand in India with strong brand loyalty and pricing power.
